Halo Frontlines

Your warthogs speed towards the enemy lines and start
opening fire at the towers. The plan has worked at stopping
the construction of the towers but a volley of plasma shots
crash into the warthogs which temporarily disables their
engines. In this time the Covenant are able to wipe out your team
with a barrage of shots. The Covenant are free to move on towards
the factory and are successful with destroying it.
