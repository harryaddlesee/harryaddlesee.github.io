Halo Frontlines

Your team rolls out to search the streets of Mombassa for the group
of Covenant. They come across a few destroyed buildings so they know
they are on the right trail. The Covenant group are on foot so your
team is easily able to catch up but they don't want to get too close
as this will give away their element of suprise. The team have a
couple of options to choose from, they can either take an aggressive
approach and attempt to deal with the group of enemies straight away
or they can take a stealthier approach by sending the 2 warthogs up
ahead to scout out the group of enemies which will allow them to
attack at the correct moment.
