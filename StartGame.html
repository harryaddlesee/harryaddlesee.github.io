Halo Frontlines

The war between Earth and the Covenant has endured the better part of the last
decade and many people have lost their lives fighting for the survival of mankind.
The Covenant have been held down in the streets of Mombassa for last few months
but new intel suggests that a group of Covenant have broken through the frontlines
and are planning an assault on a nearby munitions factory. If they are successful
with this mission the consequences could reslut in losing control of the entire city
into Covenant hands You will play as Corporal Frank O,Conner who is the leader of a highly
skilled group of marines. Your team is tasked with locating and eliminating the enemy
threat before they can make it to the Factory. It is vital that your team is successful
with this mission as many lives are on the line.

(If this is your first time playing it is recommended that you check out the About page on how to play)
