Halo Frontlines

Half of your team moves forward in the warthogs while the other
half hang back in the scorpion. As the warthogs move up they realise
there are significantly more Covenant than they had been told. With
this information your team is able to radio up the tank to move to a
position where they can try and take out the bigger enemies first as
they will cause the most threat for their vehicles. When the scorpion
gets into position your team begins raining shots at the Covenant. Some
well placed scorpion shots take out a couple of the heavy grunts which
allows your team to push up further. As your team moves up a group
ambushes one of your warthogs and ends up taking them out. You are now
faced with the decison on whether to continue your attack with only 9
marines or whether to attempt to retreat.
