How To Play:


Throughout the game there will be several instances where
you the player will be able to choose how you would like the
story to progress. You will be given a time limit to pick one of
the two options but if you do not decide in time then the top
option will be automatically picked for you. These decsions will
alter how the game progresses and you will have to pick wisely as
picking the wrong option can result in a game over.
