Halo Frontlines

Your team flies in and scowers the streets of Mombassa until they eventually
find the group of Covenant that they have been looking for. The group is
significantly larger than the intel they were provided with made out. This
shouldn't be a problem for your team as if they initiate the fight correctly
they will be able to wipe out the group with ease. The team are given two
options on how they will proceed, either they wait for night to fall and
try to silently deal with the enemy threat or they can take a more
aggressive approach to try and take out the enemy before they can cause
anymore damage.
