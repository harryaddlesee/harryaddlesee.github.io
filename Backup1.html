Halo Frontlines

Your team backs off to call for backup but every minute they
spend waiting the Covenant move closer to the munitions
factory. When the backup finally arrives you all move in
towards the enemies but you are too late as the factory
is on fire and is only a matter of time before it explodes.
Your team is forced to get shelter to protect themselves from
the explosion. Although your team survives the factory has
been brought down so your team was unsuccessful.
