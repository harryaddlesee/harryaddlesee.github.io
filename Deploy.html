Halo Frontlines

Your team has two options on how they would like to be deployed to
the enemies position. Your first option is to be air dropped near
the enemies last known position and the other is to take some armoured
and weaponised vehicles. The air drop option will allow your team to
gain a more advantageous position comapred to the other and will allow
your team to make a more undetectable approach, However the drive in
option will mean your team is in a better position if the enemy put up more
of a fight than they were expecting.
