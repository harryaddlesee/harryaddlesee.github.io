Halo Frontlines

Your team begins getting ready to head out on their mission by
suiting up and selecting their weapons that they will bring along
on the mission. Your team has a total of 12 marines that manage to
fill 2 falcons. The falcons are fitted with new technology that will
hide them from the Covenants high-tech radar systems. This will allow
the team to scout out the area without having to worry about getting
shot out of the sky.
