Halo Frontlines

Your team stays back and holds their position. Any
Covenant that get too close are obliterated by the
warthogs turrets while they wait for backup to arrive.
Eventually backup arrives and are able to help your
team eliminate the remaining Covenant. Your team is
ultimately successful in completing their mission
which will hopefully help with the removal of the
Covenant from Mobassa's streets.
